i call this one "work with what you got" because you gotta work with what you got

Press 'f' to freeze cursor color. Press 'u' to unfreeze cursor color.
Press 'c' to change cursor shape.
Press the up arrow to increase cursor size. Press the down arrow to decrease cursor size
Press 'b' to reset the background color. (This will also reset any painting already done, so choose your background color first.)

Your cursor size, shape, and color are displayed in the top left corner.
Note: Changing cursor size or shape will "erase" the drawing under it.
